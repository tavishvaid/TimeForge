Privacy policy & cookie statement

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: June 2026

1. What data we collect

TimeForge collects only the data you provide: your email address (for login), your name, schedule blocks, notes, health goals, budget information, and preferences. This data is stored securely in our database (Supabase) and is used solely to power the app's features for you.

2. How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to provide you with personalised scheduling, health tracking, financial tools, and AI-powered recommendations. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling purposes.

3. We will never sell or share your data

TimeForge does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties for commercial purposes. Your data belongs to you.

4. AI processing

When you use AI features, your schedule and preference data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API solely to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. No personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary is transmitted.

5. Cookies

TimeForge uses only essential cookies required for authentication (login sessions via Supabase). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies. No cookie consent banner is required as we only use strictly necessary cookies.

6. Data storage & security

All data is stored in Supabase with row-level security — meaning only you can access your own data. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us. We will process all deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated via the app. Continued use of TimeForge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

✦ Our commitment
TimeForge is built with privacy by design. Your personal schedule, health data, and financial information are sensitive — we treat them that way. We will never monetize your data.
